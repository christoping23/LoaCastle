Class Introduction

Meet the core Loa Castle classes and their distinct roles in party play and PvP

Azure Knight

Melee | Sword, Mace, Axe, Shield

Versatile melee frontliner who can tank or deal heavy physical damage using greatswords, dual weapons, or sword-and-shield setups.

Class Background

Azure Knights are the descendants of Undo Masochie that once filled Genoa, the Land of Destruction, with the rays of light reflected on their swords as they slashed away at their enemies. They have abandoned magic, archery, and everything else to hone their swordsmanship to their fullest potential.

Segita Hunter

Ranged | Bow, Crossbow, Dagger

Long-range damage dealer specializing in bows, crossbows, and daggers, using mobility and debuffs to control fights from a distance.

Class Background

Segita Hunters are the first ones that succeeded in establishing trade and commerce with the people of Helicita. These children of Qune, the goddess of beauty, are skilled tacticians and archers; loyal soldiers as long as they are not given commands that violate their ethics.

Incar Magician

Magic | Wand, Staff

Elemental caster with powerful fire, ice, and lightning spells, excelling at wide-area damage and crowd control in parties.

Class Background

These priestesses of Incar, also known as Zodiac, are passionately driven to acquire knowledge about magic and the world's truths. They can create magic spells by using only the Force Elemental, strictly focusing on elemental magic while despising black magic.

Vicious Summoner

Melee, Range | Twin Blade, Staff

Hybrid fighter who commands summoned creatures while using twin blades and debuffs for aggressive, hit-and-run combat.

Class Background

The summoners have existed since the age of the Alokens, long before the Incar Order was established. Their ability to summon creatures is mysterious, and despite suppression, this type of black magic is attracting more practitioners, even after the disastrous Grand Fall.

Segnale

Range | Blood Whip

Primary support class that heals, resurrects, and curses enemies, making it essential in dungeon runs and group PvP.

Class Background

Segnale are still connected to the Abyss, yet they have one foot in the human realms. They have mastered the art of the Blood Whip, and their healing abilities are praised by the wounded and the dead. Their fighting abilities cause great devastation among enemies.

Bagi Warrior

Melee | Gauntlet

Durable berserker with high defense and magic resistance, using heavy-hitting close-range skills to disrupt enemy lines.

Class Background

The Grand Fall exposed the Bagi Warriors to otherworldly contamination, granting their bodies inhumane strength and imbuing them with the parasitic Spirit of Karon. They control this spirit to unleash special abilities and maximize their physical capabilities.

Spear-wielding hybrid that delivers steady melee damage while boosting allies with powerful buffs and defensive support spells.

Class Background

Alokens have remained on the land, unlike their kin who deserted Trieste. After the Grand Fall, they settled in the Jungle of Eternity and blamed their pride for the disaster brought upon the land. They wished for peace until Karon destroyed the jungle.
