Class Introduction

Meet the core Loa Castle classes and their distinct roles in party play and PvP.

Azure Knight

Versatile melee frontliner who can tank or deal heavy physical damage using greatswords, dual weapons, or sword-and-shield setups.


Class Background

Azure Knights are the descendants of Undo Masochie that once filled Genoa, the Land of Destruction, with the rays of light reflected on their swords as they slashed away at their enemies. They have abandoned magic, archery, and everything else to hone their swordsmanship to their fullest potential and wield the power of Mother Nature.

Segita Hunter

Long-range damage dealer specializing in bows, crossbows, and daggers, using mobility and debuffs to control fights from a distance.


Class Background

Segita Hunters are the first ones that succeeded in establishing trade and commerce with the people of Helicita and the other states beyond the mountains. These children of Qune, the goddess of beauty, are skilled tacticians and archers; they are loyal soldiers for their employers as long as they are not given commands that violate their ethics.

Incar Magician

Elemental caster with powerful fire, ice, and lightning spells, excelling at wide-area damage and crowd control in parties.


Class Background

These priestesses of Incar, also known as Zodiac, are passionately driven to acquire knowledge about magic and the world's truths. They can create magic spells by using only the Force Elemental, the most basic element of nature. Incar Magicians strictly focus on elemental magic and also despise black magic, which caused the Grand Fall.

Vicious Summoner

Hybrid fighter who commands summoned creatures while using twin blades and debuffs for aggressive, hit-and-run combat.


Class Background

The summoners have existed since the age of the Alokens, long before the Incar Order was established or the use of magic was regulated by strict laws. Their ability to summon creatures is very mysterious, and the Incar Order considers their arts as black magic that must be prohibited. Despite their suppression, this type of black magic is attracting more practitioners, even after the disastrous Grand Fall.

Segnale

Primary support class that heals, resurrects, and curses enemies, making it essential in dungeon runs and group PvP.


Class Background

Segnale are still connected to the Abyss, yet they have one foot in the human realms. They have mastered the art of the Blood Whip, and their taste for blood makes them outcasts among other people. However, their healing abilities are praised and welcomed by the wounded and the dead. Their fighting abilities are also appreciated, as they can cause great devastation among the enemy with their wide range of powers.

Bagi Warrior

Durable berserker with high defense and magic resistance, using heavy-hitting close-range skills to disrupt enemy lines.


Class Background

The Grand Fall exposed the Bagi Warriors to otherworldly contamination, granting their bodies inhumane strength and imbuing them with the parasitic Spirit of Karon. Bagi Warriors use their incredible stamina and unshakable willpower to survive deadly fights. They control the Spirit of Karon within them to unleash special abilities and maximize their physical capabilities, greatly enhancing their power.

Aloken

Spear-wielding hybrid that delivers steady melee damage while boosting allies with powerful buffs and defensive support spells.


Class Background

Alokens have remained on the land, unlike their kin who deserted Trieste. After the Grand Fall, they settled in the Jungle of Eternity and blamed their pride for the disaster brought upon the land. They wished for a quiet and peaceful life until Karon, who feared the Alokens’ strength, destroyed the jungle.
